I didn’t climb a career ladder. I built one. Across 9.5 years at the largest auto transport broker in the U.S., every flagship win started the same way: no playbook, no precedent, no dedicated owner. A voice-of-the-customer program, a feedback infrastructure, a fraud detection workflow, a first-of-its-kind guaranteed service, and a UX function all went from zero to operational because I made them exist.

The numbers track it: an 18% conversion lift across roughly 30 experiments, a public rating rebuilt from 2.5 stars that helped drive a company acquisition and was later sustained at 4.7, and systems that were formalized into dedicated departments after I moved on.

These case studies include what worked, the real numbers, and honest retrospectives on what I’d do differently. If a case study reads like a highlight reel, it isn’t telling you the truth.
